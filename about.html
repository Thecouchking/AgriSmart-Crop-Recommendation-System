What is AgriSmart?

The role of a farmer is extremely important for maintaining the natural balance in the ecosystem. Many other personnel require a farmer for their survival. A farmer is an individual who produces the requisite food for others to prosper. Hence, it is extremely important for him to know what crop suits their land the most.

By means of AgriSmart, we have tried to develop a system that recommends the most suitable crop for the farmer. He would be required to enter certain parameters like nitrogen, phosphorus, moisture, potassium, etc., and the system would then recommend the most suitable crop.

Through this project, we have primarily used three main models -Random Forest, Naive Bayes and XG Boost- and evaluated their accuracy levels to choose the best among them. The algorithm employed in this project is Machine Learning. The specific technique used is Regression, i.e., predicting the crop based on input parameters.

We hope to make a positive impact through this software and have worked diligently to achieve the same.
